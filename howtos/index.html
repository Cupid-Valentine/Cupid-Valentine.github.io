BookIt Howtos

  • How to install BookIt for my browser?

    Just download the extension for your browser into a file in your disk, and then drag the file into your browser to install it.After installation completed, an icon with a KiWi bird will appear in the toolbar area of your browser,and you know you have got it.

  • How to start the conversion ?

    Just click the Kiwi bird on the toolbar area of your browser, a window will popup to guide you step by step to do the things.

  • How to uninstall BookIt?

    For chrome users,you could uninstall BookIt via menu item: More tools->extensions, this will open a page which will list all the extensions you have installed ,then you can click the "Remove" button of the extension to remove it.

  • How to get more trial hours?

    Each unregistered user will get 2 trial hours of the BookIt service,you can try to convert web pages in that 2 hours after you first installation of the browser extension. Then if you wish to continue using our service,you can register on the site to get another 8 hours. So everyone got 10 trial hours total freely. After that ,you can choose to order a membership package to continue using BookIt.

  • How to put the ebook generated into my Kindle for reading?

    The ebook generated by BookIt was in EPUB format which is not supported by Amazon's kindle device, so you need to convert it into MOBI or AZW3 format which are supported by Kindle. We recommend calibre as the option.

  • How to convert a web page without ToC block.

    If you wish to convert a page without ToC, you can click skip in the step of "choosing ToC block" of the BookIt wizard,and the resulting EPUB will have an empty ToC. Or you can specify the ToC manually in the step of "review the ToC" step, enter a name as a chapter title, and an URL as the address of the content of this chapter,repeating this until all the chapters defined.
