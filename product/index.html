Why & What

BookIt is an online service to convert a web page with TOC (Table of Contents) block into a EPUB for you to read on your eReader device later(Amazon Kindle, Kobo, IOS/Android mobile phone etc).
BookIt can analyze the HTMLs and generate the ToC for your EPUB automatically , it then traverse all the links in the TOC to fetch the linked web pages, filter out the irrelevant Web components ( header, menu, footer, adv., etc.) and pack all pages into a valid ebook in EPUB format.
Consisted of a frontend browser extension with background service, BookIt provides a light-weight solution to convert web pages into an ebook with just a few clicks.

Feature

A new era of reading delivery

Accuracy
Text contents of the web page are filtered and common web page components such as header\footer\adv. etc are excluded to give you a clean view.
Customizability
Metadatas of the book can be modified ,the TOC can be adjusted to reflect what you want.
Automaticity
it analyze the web page automatically to find the ToC block at best as we can ,also it parse the ToC block automatically to generate the TOC of the book.
Completeness
All links in the ToC blocks are processed to get a full-edged book without omission.
Reproducibility
Users can regenerate their book of a page any time after their first usage,even if the web site host the pages are redesigned.
History data cache
Books are cached to speed up downloading in 30 days from the time they are generated.
